About Me

Ivelise Sola grew up in Havana, Cuba. Like all Cubans, she loves music, food, and dancing, but unlike most, she was actually a classical dancer turned software developer (work in progress). She likes traveling, she has visited different cities across the US and plans to visit Europe in the near future.


Since coming to the US in 2015, Ivelise has focused on learning the language to accomplish the goal of becoming a software developer. After working as a computer security specialist in Cuba, she felt right at home in the tech industry.

She decided to enroll in the UNCC Coding Bootcamp and start her path to becoming a web developer. She's determined to succeed in this endeavor and perfect her coding skills in these six months and beyond.
